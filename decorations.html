Some Greener Alternatives You Can Explore

  • Flower Petal Confetti: Instead of plastic confetti, use dried flower petals or fresh leaves.
  • Reusable Fabric Drapes: Replace plastic banners with organic cotton or linen drapes.
  • Potted Plants & Herbs: Use potted plants instead of cut flowers; guests can take them home after the event.
  • Biodegradable Tableware: Decorate tables with compostable plates and wooden cutlery.
  • Solar-Powered Lights: Opt for solar fairy lights instead of electricity-powered string lights.
  • Bamboo & Jute Decor: Use bamboo structures and jute decorations instead of synthetic materials.
  • Reclaimed Wood Centerpieces: Use upcycled wood pieces for rustic, elegant centerpieces.
