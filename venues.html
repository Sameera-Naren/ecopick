Sustainable Settings for Unforgettable Events

  • Choose a Naturally Lit Venue – Reduce electricity use by hosting events in open-air or well-lit spaces.
  • Go Decoration-Free or Use Reusables like reusable table settings and linens
  • Ban Single-Use Plastics – Provide reusable or biodegradable alternatives for all event essentials.
  • Set Up Recycling & Compost Stations – Clearly label bins to ensure proper waste disposal.
  • Use Digital Invitations & Signage – Skip paper and go for e-invites and digital displays
  • Opt for Water Refill Stations – Avoid plastic water bottles by providing refillable dispensers.
  • Reduce Energy Usage – Choose venues with solar power, energy-efficient lighting, or minimal electricity needs.
